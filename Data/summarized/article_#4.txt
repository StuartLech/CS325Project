Title: **Oscars 2024: Global Cinema Triumphs, "Barbie" Snubs Simmer**

The Academy Award nominations for 2024 reflect a globalized film industry, with several non-English language films earning recognition. Notably, "Oppenheimer" and "Barbie" are frontrunners with 13 and 8 nominations, respectively. Despite its commercial success, "Barbie" faced major snubs, with star Margot Robbie and director Greta Gerwig not receiving nominations. The Academy recognized new talent with nominations for "American Fiction" and "Past Lives," as well as veterans like Martin Scorsese and John Williams.
