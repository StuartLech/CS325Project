Title: **Spielberg's Fiery Warning: Extremism's Scorching Embers Ignite the Flames of History**

Steven Spielberg warns of rising extremism and its potential consequences, citing the echoes of history in current events. He emphasizes the importance of combating antisemitism and hate through storytelling and documentation, highlighting the Shoah Foundation's mission to preserve the testimonies of Holocaust survivors and witnesses. Spielberg expressed concern about the spread of extremist views, particularly on college campuses, and urged the need for empathy, resilience, and the pursuit of peace and understanding.
