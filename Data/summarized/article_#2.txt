Title: "Ruffalo's Sizzling Star: Jennifer Garner Spills Tea on 'Thriller' Anxiety and Hollywood Heroics"

Mark Ruffalo was honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Jennifer Garner, his "13 Going on 30" co-star, praised him for his acting and character. Garner joked about Ruffalo's anxiety during the "Thriller" dance rehearsals for the film, which is celebrating its 20th anniversary. Ruffalo, nominated for an Oscar for "Poor Things," was compared to screen legends by Garner.
