Title: "Super Bowl LVIII: A Symphony of Patriotism, Gospel, and Rock 'n' Roll Excess"

Reba McEntire, Andra Day, and Post Malone performed at Super Bowl LVIII. McEntire sang the national anthem, Day sang "Lift Every Voice and Sing," and Malone performed an acoustic rendition of "America the Beautiful." McEntire has been singing the national anthem for 50 years and got her singing contract after performing it in 1974. Day emphasized the "gospel element" of "Lift Every Voice and Sing," known as the Black national anthem. Malone has a Kansas City Chiefs tattoo and a collaboration with Taylor Swift on the upcoming song "Tortured Poets Department."
