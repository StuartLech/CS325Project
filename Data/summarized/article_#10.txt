Title: "Jerry's Cereal-ous Adventure: Netflix Serves Up 'Unfrosted' with a Scorching Star-Studded Pastry Throwdown!"

Jerry Seinfeld is directing and starring in "Unfrosted: The Pop-Tart Story," an upcoming Netflix film about the iconic breakfast item's origins. The film, set in the 1960s, features a star-studded cast including Melissa McCarthy, Hugh Grant, Amy Schumer, and Bill Burr. It follows rival cereal corporations Kellogg's and Post as they compete to create a game-changing pastry. Seinfeld, known for his cereal-eating antics in "Seinfeld," leads the charge in this breakfast-related tale. "Unfrosted" premieres on Netflix on May 3.
